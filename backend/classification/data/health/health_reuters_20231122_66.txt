The global fight against antimicrobial resistance (AMR) is at a critical juncture. The overuse and misuse of antibiotics in humans and agriculture have accelerated the rise of drug-resistant bacteria, rendering common infections untreatable. This silent pandemic threatens to undermine modern medicine. The article details international efforts to promote antibiotic stewardship, develop new treatments, and improve surveillance, stressing the urgency of a coordinated global response to this growing threat.
