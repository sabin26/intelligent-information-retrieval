Scientists are making progress in unraveling the complex mysteries of Long Covid, a condition that affects millions with persistent symptoms after a COVID-19 infection. Research is beginning to identify potential causes, including the persistence of the virus in the body, autoimmune responses, and microclots in the blood. While a definitive cure remains elusive, this growing understanding is paving the way for the development of diagnostic tools and potential treatments to help those suffering from this debilitating condition.
