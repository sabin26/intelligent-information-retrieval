The cost of health insurance premiums continues to rise, placing a growing financial strain on American families and businesses. Factors driving the increases include the rising price of medical care and prescription drugs, as well as consolidation in the hospital and insurance industries. The article discusses how these rising premiums impact household budgets and the difficult choices people face, such as opting for high-deductible plans or forgoing coverage altogether. This trend remains a central issue in the U.S. healthcare debate.
