Intermittent fasting has gained popularity as a weight-loss strategy, and scientific research is beginning to uncover the mechanisms behind its potential benefits. Studies suggest that fasting can impact metabolism, improve insulin sensitivity, and trigger cellular repair processes. However, the science is still evolving, and the effectiveness of intermittent fasting can vary greatly among individuals. The article explores the different types of fasting, the current state of the research, and what people should consider before trying it.
