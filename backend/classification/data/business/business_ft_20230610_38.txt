The era of the tech 'unicorn'—private startups valued at over $1 billion—may be coming to an end. The recent downturn in the technology sector and the more challenging fundraising environment have led to a sharp decrease in the number of new unicorns being created. Many existing unicorns have seen their valuations slashed. The market is now favoring sustainable growth and profitability over the high-risk, high-reward model that defined the last decade's tech boom.
