'Buy Now, Pay Later' (BNPL) services are facing increased scrutiny from financial regulators around the world. Concerns are growing about the potential for these services to encourage consumers to take on unmanageable debt. Regulators are considering new rules that would treat BNPL providers more like traditional lenders, requiring affordability checks and clearer disclosure of terms. The move aims to protect consumers from the risks associated with these popular but largely unregulated financing options.
