As a political campaign enters its final weeks, this article outlines the key dynamics and factors to watch. This 'final push' period is characterized by intense activity, including a flurry of advertising, rallies, and get-out-the-vote efforts. The focus shifts to mobilizing core supporters and persuading the small number of undecided voters in key battleground areas. The analysis highlights which candidate has momentum and what last-minute events could potentially alter the outcome of the race.
