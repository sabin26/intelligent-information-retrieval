Election officials and security experts have identified misinformation and the malicious use of artificial intelligence as top threats to the 2024 election. There are growing concerns about the potential for AI-generated 'deepfakes' and other synthetic media to deceive voters and spread false narratives. These technologies make it easier to create convincing but fake audio and video content. Efforts are underway to develop detection tools and educate the public to counter this emerging threat to democratic integrity.
