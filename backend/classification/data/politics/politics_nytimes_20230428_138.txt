Big Tech companies are facing intense scrutiny in a series of congressional hearings focused on antitrust issues. Lawmakers from both parties are questioning the market power and competitive practices of companies like Google, Amazon, and Apple. The hearings are part of a broader effort by the government, including the Justice Department and the FTC, to rein in the power of these tech giants. The debate centers on whether these companies have become monopolies that stifle competition and harm consumers.
