The Supreme Court is set to have a significant impact on the 2024 election, with several major cases on its docket that touch on politically sensitive issues. Rulings on cases related to voting rights, administrative power, and social issues could energize voters and become major campaign topics. The Court's decisions have the potential to reshape the legal and political landscape just as the election cycle intensifies. Both parties are closely watching the Court and preparing to use its rulings to their political advantage.
