Leaders of the Group of Seven (G7) nations met to confront a range of pressing global issues, including economic security, the war in Ukraine, and competition with China. The summit focused on coordinating their strategies to de-risk, but not decouple, from the Chinese economy. They also pledged continued, unwavering support for Ukraine and discussed ways to enforce sanctions against Russia. The meeting highlighted the efforts of leading democracies to present a united front on major international challenges.
