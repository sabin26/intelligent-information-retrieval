The shifting U.S. economy is having major political implications. Issues like persistent inflation, rising interest rates, and the resilience of the job market are top of mind for voters. The Biden administration is trying to convince the public that its economic policies are working, a message it calls 'Bidenomics.' Republicans, meanwhile, are blaming the administration's spending for high prices. Voters' perceptions of their own financial situation will be a key factor in the upcoming elections.
